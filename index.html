About CivicTrack

Smart civic management for every citizen

CivicTrack bridges the gap between citizens and municipal authorities — making urban governance faster, transparent, and accountable.

  • Report civic issues with location, photo, and description
  • Receive a unique tracking ID for every complaint
  • Authorities review and assign resolution tasks instantly
  • Get notified when your issue is resolved

Process

How CivicTrack works

Four simple steps from reporting a problem to seeing it resolved in your city.

1

Report Issue

Submit your location, description, and a photo of the problem.

2

Complaint Registered

System generates a unique tracking ID instantly.

3

Authority Review

Municipal staff analyze and assign the right team to fix it.

4

Issue Resolved

Status updated and you get notified when it's done.
