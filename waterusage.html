📋 Penalty FAQs

When is the penalty issued?
Penalties are calculated at the end of each calendar month and appear on your next bill.
Can I appeal a penalty?
Yes. File an appeal within 15 days of issue by visiting the municipal office or calling 1800-XXX-XXXX.
What if I don't pay on time?
A late fee of ₹50 is added after the due date. Continued non-payment may result in supply suspension.
How is usage measured?
Usage is recorded by your household water meter. Readings are taken on the 1st of each month.
🌿 Conservation Tips
🚿

Shorter Showers

Reducing shower time by 2 minutes saves up to 20L per day. Switch to a low-flow shower head.

Saves ~600L/month
🪣

Reuse Washing Water

Collect washing machine rinse water and reuse it for mopping or flushing.

Saves ~100L/month
🔧

Fix Leaking Taps

A dripping tap wastes 20L per day. Report pipe leaks on CivicTrack immediately.

Saves ~600L/month
🌱

Water Plants Wisely

Water plants in the morning or evening to reduce evaporation. Use a watering can, not a hose.

Saves ~80L/month
🍽️

Wash Dishes Smartly

Fill the sink rather than running tap water. Only run dishwashers with full loads.

Saves ~150L/month
🚽

Check Your Flush

Place a bottle in your cistern to reduce flush volume. Use dual-flush if available.

Saves ~200L/month
