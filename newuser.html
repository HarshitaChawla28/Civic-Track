💡 Tips for New Members

📸

Always attach a photo

Complaints with photos are resolved 2x faster. Even a blurry photo from your phone helps authorities confirm the issue.

⚡ 2× faster resolution
📍

Be specific with location

Mention a nearby landmark — "near the red gate of Sector 12 park" works better than just the street name.

📍 Helps teams find it fast
💧

Check your water usage before the end of each month. Going over 300L triggers a fine — stay in the safe zone.

💰 Avoid ₹200–₹700 penalty
