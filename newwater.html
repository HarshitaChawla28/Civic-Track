💡 Tips to Stay Within Your Limit
🚿

Time your showers

Each minute in the shower uses about 10L. A 5-minute shower = 50L. Set a timer.

Saves ~300L/month
🔧

Check for leaks first

If your meter reading is high with no obvious use, you may have a hidden pipe leak. Report on CivicTrack.

Prevents penalty
📊

Coming back here daily keeps you aware. Most overages happen gradually without anyone noticing.
